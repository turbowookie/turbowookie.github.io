Problem

Today, there are several ways to listen to music online, through services such as Spotify, Google Music, Pandora, and the like. However, while all of them are streaming services, none of them let you stream with other people concurrently.

Solution

Turbo Wookie is a collaborative music jukebox. Turbo Wookie lets a group of individuals (we'll call them friends) collaboratively create a playlist and listen to it at the same time.

Why

There are many times when a group of people might want to listen to the same music (or any kind of audio content) at the same time.

One such instance is, with the current remote working hype, an office could start an instance of Turbo Wookie and let remote workers connect, helping foster a sense of community.
